Event Planning and Design for Western Weddings, Rodeos, and more.

Western Gold prides themselves in crafting homegrown, authentic events from the ground up. This small business, owned and managed by Halie Cameron, is headquartered in Bozeman, Montana but much of her work is out of office and across the country for destinations events on large, family owned ranches. Raised as the daughter of a bullrider and rancher, Halie understands the needs of those in the agriculture community and strives to provide high quality, traditional events.
